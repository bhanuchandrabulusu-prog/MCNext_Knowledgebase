## Strengthening Campaign Analysis

The newly provided dashboards allow marketers to:

- **Identify Failures:** Delve into the specific metrics to uncover the reasons behind email delivery failures.
- **Compare Campaigns:** Perform comparative analyses of high-performing and low-performing campaigns.
- **Plan Improvements:** Formulate effective improvement plans based on detailed data.

Additionally, each campaign record is now integrated with a “Deliverability Dashboard”, which provides an at-a-glance view of the delivery rates for emails sent in each campaign. This feature helps quickly identify challenges and implement precise improvement actions.

## Advancing Content Analysis

Marketers can now directly track the performance of emails, SMS messages, WhatsApp messages, tracking links, and landing pages through their respective content records. This provides several key benefits:

- **Understanding Audience Engagement:** Analyze reactions to individual pieces of content in greater detail.
- **Evaluating Dynamic Content:** Assess the effectiveness of variations (excluding evaluations for repeater components).

The “Marketing Performance” tab is accessible from the properties section of each content record. This enhances transparency and efficiency in marketing activities while providing valuable insights to inform future campaigns.

## Permission Set

To access these features, the following permission set is required:

- **“Tableau Next Included App Business User”**

Only users with this permission set can view and access the “Marketing Performance” tab.

## Troubleshooting Display Errors

In some environments, display errors may occur. These issues can often be resolved by reinstalling the “Marketing Performance” feature. Notably, reinstalling does not affect existing data.

### Reinstallation Steps:

**1. Remove the Current Installation**

- Navigate to the settings screen, search for “Marketing Performance”, select the “Marketing Performance” tab, and click the “Delete” button in the top-right corner of the screen.

**2. Reinstall the Feature**

- Once the deletion is complete, the “Install” button will become active. Click it to reinstall the feature.

## Important Notes

Before using Marketing Performance, please keep the following points in mind:

- The dashboards cannot be customized in terms of content or layout.
- Using this feature will consume Data Cloud credits. If you do not require these analytics capabilities, it is recommended to refrain from installing the feature.

Starting March 6, 2024, **Google has begun requesting two consent settings from Marketing Cloud Advertising users (advertisers) in the European Economic Area (EEA)** when uploading data for Customer Match.

[## FAQs About the EU user consent policy for Customer Match upload partners

### This article is for customers who use Customer Match partners to upload data for users in the European Economic Area

support.google.com](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/14310715?source=post_page-----b0ec32fe0ef9---------------------------------------)

The functionality to submit these to Google is **already implemented in the Marketing Cloud Advertising interface and settings are configurable**.

As of now, **users outside of the EEA are not subject to this “Consent Policy”, so it is recognized as “not required” for the time being**.

However, since it is uncertain how this may change in the future, **if you choose to select something, it is recommended to set “User Data Consent” and “Personalization Consent” to “Granted”**.

- *If the consent status is set to “Unspecified”, it will be considered as no consent, and data from such EEA users will not be available for personalization of ads.*
- *If data linked before March 6, 2024, is being updated in the content of that audience, it may be advisable to move to the Marketing Cloud Advertising interface and set it to “Granted”.*
- *Salesforce Customer Support also recommends setting “Granted”* ***for all Marketing Cloud Advertising users (advertisers) in all regions****.*

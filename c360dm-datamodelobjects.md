# Legacy DMOs | Model Data in Data 360 | Data 360 DMO and Mapping Guide

**Source:** https://developer.salesforce.com/docs/data/data-cloud-dmo-mapping/guide/c360dm-datamodelobjects.html

---

Data model objects (DMOs) organize and harmonize your data in Data 360. They’re categorized into standard (std__ prefix) and legacy (ssot__ prefix) types. Standard DMOs are flexible, work consistently across all your environments, and receive regular field updates to expand your functional capabilities. For more information, see Standard DMOs.

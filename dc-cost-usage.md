# Cost and Usage | Get Started with Data 360 Development | Data 360 Developer Guide

**Source:** https://developer.salesforce.com/docs/data/data-cloud-dev/guide/dc-cost-usage.html

---

Data storage in Data 360 is metered. Service usage is metered and includes records processed, queried, or analyzed, and segmentation and activation. Also, the number of ad audiences counts against your service usage. For more information, see Data 360 Billable Usage Types in Salesforce Help. Also, see Data 360 Limits and Guidelines.

For more information on how usage is billed, refer to your contract or contact your account executive.

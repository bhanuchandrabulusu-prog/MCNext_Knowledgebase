Salesforce has announced the release of a new pricing model for Agentforce, called **Flex Credits**. This update aims to empower businesses to make strategic and flexible investments while unlocking the limitless potential of digital labor.

### What is Agentforce?

Agentforce is Salesforce’s autonomous AI agent platform designed to enhance operational efficiency and improve customer experiences for businesses.

This platform enables AI agents to operate autonomously — just like humans — providing round-the-clock support for employees and customers. As an evolving domain, Agentforce is set to expand its capabilities even further in the near future.

### What is Flex Credits?

Previously, Agentforce followed a usage-based pricing model of **$2 per conversation**. The new **Flex Credits** model, however, shifts the billing to be based on the **number of actions performed** by Agentforce.

### How Does the Pricing Work?

- **20 credits per action** (base price: $0.1).
- If fewer actions are required, costs can be lower than before.
- Conversely, higher action volumes incur costs proportional to their value.

**What is an action?**  
An action refers to specific tasks assigned to an AI agent by a business, such as:

- **Answer Questions with Knowledge**
- **Create a To-Do**
- **Create a Campaign From a Brief** (for Marketing Cloud Next)
- **Draft Content** (for Marketing Cloud Next)

Agents strictly execute tasks based on these predefined actions and will not perform unrelated operations.

Additionally, **no charges are incurred for conversations where no actions are executed**. The execution of actions is determined by analyzing conversation content and leveraging a reasoning engine.

For instance:

- One action = $0.1 (20 credits)
- Three actions = $0.3 (60 credits)

### Options and Considerations

Businesses must choose between the new **Flex Credits** model and the traditional conversation-based pricing. Both models cannot coexist within the same organization.

Transitioning from the traditional model to Flex Credits is possible, but organizations are advised to carefully evaluate which model suits their use case.

### Managing Flex Credits

- **Purchase Units**: Sold in increments of 100,000 credits ($500).
- **Credit Management**: Utilize Salesforce Digital Wallet to track, forecast, and manage credits.
- **Scalable Strategy**: Ideal for starting with small pilot projects and scaling successful use cases.

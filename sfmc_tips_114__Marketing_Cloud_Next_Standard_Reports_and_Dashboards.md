**Marketing Cloud Next (Growth & Advanced Edition)** offers not only the ability to create custom reports but also a variety of pre-configured standard reports and dashboards.

This article explores how to access these features and provides an overview of their functionality.

### Preparation

Before using the standard reports and dashboards, ensure that the following analytics packages are installed:

- **Marketing Engagement Analytics Package**
- **SMS Analytics Package**
- **Landing Pages and Forms Analytics Package**
- **Flow Report Analytics Package**

You can access these settings by navigating to **Setup > Marketing Cloud > Analytics**.

### How to Access Standard Reports and Dashboards

Accessing standard reports and dashboards is simple:

**1. Navigate to the “Analytics” tab.**

**2. Use the search bar** to locate specific reports or dashboards by entering their names, which will be introduced later in this article.

## Standard Reports and Dashboard Types

## 1. Email Engagement Reports (V1 / V2)

The Email Engagement Reports folder is grouped with reports tailored for email marketing. It includes two versions: V1 and V2. Below are the key reports available:

### V1 Reports:

- **All Email Activities:** Displays engagement metrics based on flow names. Metrics include click-through rate, sends, delivery rate, bounce rate, open rate, click rate, and unsubscribe rate.
- **Email Sends by Status:** Compares “Sent” and “Not Sent” statuses within email send records.
- **KPIs for Email Engagement:** Displays engagement metrics based on the email element names within flows. Metrics include click-through rate, sends, delivery rate, bounce rate, open rate, click rate, and unsubscribe rate.
- **Sent Email Outcomes:** Shows the outcomes of bounces and unsubscribes.
- **Top Campaigns by Click-through Rate:** Displays metrics such as sends, clicks, bounces, and click-through rate, based on campaign names.
- **Total Email Activities:** Tracks and displays metrics such as sends, opens, and bounces, categorized by activity date.

### V2 Reports:

- **Email Send Outcomes:** Displays outcomes such as sent, not sent, opened, clicked, bounced, and unsubscribed, based on the email element names within flows. This report includes detailed information such as email addresses and recipient names, offering more granularity compared to the V1 report.
- **Individual Email Engagement:** Displays daily engagement metrics based on recipient names. Caution is advised, as the report can become extensive without appropriate filtering.
- **V2 Email Deliverability KPIs:** Displays engagement metrics such as sends, not sent, bounces, and delivery rates, categorized by CMS content names and email element names within flows.
- **V2 Email Performance KPIs:** Displays engagement metrics such as sends, bounces, open rate, click rate, bounce rate, and unsubscribe rate. These metrics are categorized by CMS content names, campaign names, and email element names within flows.

## 2. Email Engagement Dashboard (V1 / V2)

Dashboards provide a high-level overview of key metrics for email marketing, including filters for date ranges, campaigns, flows, segments, and email messages.

- **V1 Dashboards**: Built on the functionality of V1 reports.

- **V2 Dashboards**: More advanced, leveraging insights from V2 reports.

Both versions quickly display essential email KPIs such as open rates, click rates, bounces, and unsubscribes. They also offer campaign-level insights, including click-through rates and not sent percentages.

## 3. SMS Engagement Reports

A folder dedicated to reports for SMS marketing. These reports are available only when the SMS channel is enabled. Without enabling this channel, the folder remains inaccessible, so the exact contents are unknown in my environment.

## 4. SMS Engagement Dashboard

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of standard SMS marketing KPIs. Filters include date ranges, campaigns, flows, segments, and SMS messages. It becomes accessible only when the SMS channel is enabled.

## 5. Forms Engagement Reports

A folder focused on form-related KPIs. Key reports include:

- **All Form Activities:** Displays the number of form submissions based on published form names.
- **Form Submission Over Time:** Displays the number of submissions for each form, categorized by the submission date.
- **KPIs for Form Engagement:** Displays the number of form submissions based on the names of landing pages where the forms are embedded.

## 6. Form Engagement Dashboard

These dashboards provide insights into signup forms, allowing filtering by date ranges and landing page titles. Metrics include the number of signups per landing page, displayed over time.

## 7. Landing Page Engagement Reports

This folder is tailored for landing page performance. Reports include:

- **All Landing Pages Activities:** Displays views, link clicks, and form submissions based on the names of published landing pages.
- **Landing Page Activity:** Displays views and link clicks, categorized by the activity date on the landing page.
- **Landing Pages Top Browsers:** Displays the number and percentage of views for each landing page by browser used (e.g., views on Chrome). Note that not all browsers are captured.
- **Landing Pages Top Devices:** Displays the number and percentage of views for each landing page by device used (e.g., views on Android). Note that not all devices are captured.
- **KPIs for Landing Page Engagement:** Displays views, link clicks, form submissions, click rate, and form submission rate based on the names of published landing pages.
- **Landing Page Engagement By Source:** Displays form views based on the URLs of pages users visited immediately before accessing the landing page. This helps identify which pages are driving the most traffic.

## 8. Landing Page Engagement Dashboard

These dashboards focus on landing page metrics, such as click-through rates, views, and browser usage percentages. Filters include date ranges and landing page titles.

## Final Thoughts

The out-of-the-box reports in Marketing Cloud Growth & Advanced Editions can be overwhelming due to their volume. It’s recommended to use these reports as templates and customize them to suit your business needs.

Explore all standard reports to identify the ones that best align with your operations.

# Overview of 5 Flow templates ready to be used in Marketing Cloud Next

**Source:** https://the-agentic-marketer.com/marketing-cloud-next-deep-dives/5-flow-templates

---

**Salesforce made 5 Flow templates available, that you can easily create as starting points, including all the associated Emails. There may be some new Flow templates added in the future, to find them, from Marketing Cloud, *Flows > +New and search for Marketing in Search Automation.* You should see the five following templates:**

- Email #1: Event Registration Confirmation

- Email #3: Event One Day Reminder
- Email #4: Event Attended Follow-Up

## Final Thoughts

Some of these scenarios are triggered by a Form Submission, others by a Segment update, we could also easily adapt them by leveraging [Trigger a reaction on click or open event](/marketing-cloud-next-deep-dives/trigger-flow-click-open-event/). Those 5 scenarios are very starting points for standard B2B use-cases, which you should definitely have a looked at, as they gather a lot of best practices, among which:

- Email copies are straight to the point, with one clear CTA
- Flows are basically drip programs, augmented with engagement checks

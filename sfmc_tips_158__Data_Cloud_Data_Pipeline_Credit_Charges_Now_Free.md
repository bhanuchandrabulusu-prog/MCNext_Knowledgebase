Previously, I posted an article about the credit charges for Data Cloud **Data Pipelines** (ingestion into data streams), noting that fees based on the volume of data processed from **Salesforce internal data only** would be significantly reduced.

**Salesforce internal data** refers to data from the following tools:

- Salesforce CRM
- Salesforce Commerce Cloud
- Salesforce Marketing Cloud Engagement
- Salesforce Marketing Cloud Personalization

Now, according to the newly updated credit table — and with confirmation from Salesforce — **ingesting Salesforce internal data is completely free**.

It is unclear from what time the imported data will be subject to this, but it will apply to data from **August 11, 2025**, onward.

\*There will be no retroactive measures.

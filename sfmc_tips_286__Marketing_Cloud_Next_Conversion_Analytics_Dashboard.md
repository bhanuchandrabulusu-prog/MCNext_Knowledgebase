As a new feature introduced in [**Spring ’26**](/@marketingcloudtips/marketing-cloud-next-spring-26-release-highlights-24c0c804b0cb) for **Marketing Cloud Next Growth & Advanced Edition**, the **Conversion Analytics Dashboard** enables a deeper understanding of campaign performance.

You can access it from **Performance > “Conversions”** within each campaign.

*This feature has been released as part of* ***Marketing Performance*** *(a component of Tableau Next). The dashboard itself cannot be edited.*

In addition to traditional metrics such as open rates and click-through rates, it is now possible to directly view real business outcomes — such as **form submissions** and **order completions** — within the campaign record.  
 You can visualize how Email and SMS deliveries lead to conversions through a **30-day conversion window**.

Furthermore, by applying **First Touch** or **Last Touch** attribution models, you can analyze which messages most effectively guided customers to conversion.

This allows you to go beyond simple engagement metrics and clearly understand how your initiatives contribute to actual business outcomes, leading to more precise optimization of your marketing strategy.

To enable this feature, go to the **Marketing Performance** page.

- After turning on the toggle, it will be installed within a few minutes.
- If you are unable to enable the toggle on the screen, try deleting Marketing Performance once and then try again.
- Once you start using it, **Data 360 credits** will be consumed.
